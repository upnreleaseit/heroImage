Discover St. Lawrence County

Where the Adirondacks Meet the Mighty St. Lawrence River

Explore Now

Thousand Islands Experience

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Thousand Islands region right here in St. Lawrence County. Take a scenic boat tour, explore historic Boldt Castle, or simply enjoy the pristine waters of the St. Lawrence River.

Popular activities include kayaking, fishing, and island hopping. The region offers stunning views year-round, from summer sunsets to winter ice formations.

Adirondack Adventures

Venture into the northwestern edge of the Adirondack Park, where pristine wilderness awaits. From hiking trails at Cranberry Lake to cross-country skiing in Canton, outdoor enthusiasts will find endless opportunities for adventure.

Visit local favorites like Robert Moses State Park, or discover hidden gems on the trails near Colton and Parishville-Hopkinton.

Local Culture & Community

Experience the charm of North Country communities. Visit Canton's vibrant Main Street, explore the shops and restaurants of Potsdam, or attend local events in Massena and surrounding towns.

From farmers markets to cultural festivals, St. Lawrence County offers authentic small-town hospitality combined with rich history and natural beauty.
